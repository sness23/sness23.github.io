<h3 class="title">after effects</h3>

<p>Straight & Pre-Multiplied Alpha Channels</p>

<p>Alpha channels can be saved as straight or premultiplied.</p>

<p>Straight (also known as unmatted) relies solely on the alpha channel to determine opacity.</p>

<p>Premultiplied (also known as matted) store information in the alpha channel, but also factor in transparency information into the R, G & B channels. In premultiplied files, the nicely feathered semitransparent edges will become mixed with the background color.</p>

<p>Programs like After Effects interpret alpha channels as either straight or premutliplied. Using the wrong interpretation results in undesirable white fringing or halos around the perimeter of the alpha.</p>

<p>From <a href="http://www.indiana.edu/~telecom/faculty/krause/t354/week3.html">here</a></p>

<h3 class="title">LESSON 26 from Merging with</h3>

<p>LESSON 26 from Merging with Siva </p>

<p>Be Like the River Water </p>

<p>You can plainly see that we have to go into the subconscious basement and <br />
straighten it out, if need be, by letting go and becoming free. That's <br />
easy to say. It's a little more difficult to do. Why? Because the basement <br />
took time to fill, and it takes time to clean. If we were to straighten <br />
out the subconscious basement too fast, that would not be good. It would <br />
be going against a natural law. It would be like pouring hot water on <br />
plants, as they do commercially, to make them bloom quickly. We must <br />
not force natural laws, so we take time in our spiritual unfoldment. The <br />
more time you are willing to take, the less pressure you have on yourself <br />
and the faster you will attain a permanent enlightenment. </p>

<p>Let's look again at the river of life as it flows into the sea, and <br />
again relate that to ourselves and see ourselves letting go of the river <br />
banks, merging ourselves into this river, flowing with it and realizing <br />
ourselves as the essence of life. Let us not worry about the past ever <br />
again. Do not even think about the past. Face everything that comes up <br />
in the light of the present, not in the darkness of the past. </p>

<p>Be like the river water. Water flows freely anywhere, easily finding its <br />
way around rocks and trees. Be pliable in your life, moving in rhythm with <br />
life. Let go of everything that blocks the river of life's energy. Watch <br />
your thinking and be careful of your thoughts. Judge every action that <br />
you make, judge every word you speak, with this law: "Is it true? Is it <br />
kind? Is it helpful? Is it necessary?" Become your own kind judge and <br />
make each second a day of judgment, for each second is really a day, <br />
if you live life fully. If you live completely each second, you will <br />
experience many days inside each twenty-four hours. </p>

<p>Be free from life's attachments and don't allow any more negative <br />
attachments to occur in your life. Loosen yourself--be free. Attachments <br />
bring all sorts of complications. Freedom brings no complications <br />
at all. So, that is what we have to do, recreate our lives each <br />
second. Become affectionately detached and manifest a greater love <br />
through action. Selfless service to mankind makes you free in the world <br />
of mortals. Measure yourself objectively with the river of life and merge <br />
with it into the sea of life. Let your service to mankind begin at home <br />
and radiate out to the world. Begin at home, with those closest to you, <br />
before venturing out among friends and strangers. Let your example <br />
be your first teaching. Be free from the past; abide in the present; <br />
detach yourself from the future; and live in the eternal now.</p>

<h3 class="title">There are two kinds of</h3>

<p>There are two kinds of intelligence: one acquired,<br />
as a child in school memorizes facts and concepts<br />
from books and from what the teacher says,<br />
collecting information from the traditional sciences<br />
as well as from the new sciences.</p>

<p>With such intelligence you rise in the world.<br />
You get ranked ahead or behind others<br />
in regard to your competence in retaining<br />
information.  You stroll with this intelligence<br />
in and out of fields of knowledge, getting always more<br />
marks on your preserving tablets.</p>

<p>There is another kind of tablet,<br />
one already completed and preserved inside you.<br />
A spring overflowing its springbox.  A freshness<br />
in the center of the chest.  This other intelligence<br />
does not turn yellow or stagnate.  It's fluid,<br />
and it doesn't move from outside to inside<br />
through the conduits of plumbing-learning.</p>

<p>This second knowing is a fountainhead<br />
from within you, moving out.</p>

<p>           -- Version by Coleman Barks<br />
              "The Essential Rumi"<br />
              HarperSanFrancisco, 1995</p>
