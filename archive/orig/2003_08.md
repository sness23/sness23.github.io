<h3 class="title">On my keyboard, backspace and</h3>

<p>On my keyboard, backspace and "/" are right next to each other.  Xemacs uses Ctrl-Alt-/ to indent a region, and X11 usually uses Ctrl-Alt-Backspace to kill the Xserver.  I just got bit by this again.  </p>

<p>The solution is to put the following into your /etc/X11/XF86Config:<br />
<pre><br />
Section "ServerFlags"<br />
    DontZap<br />
EndSection<br />
</pre></p>
